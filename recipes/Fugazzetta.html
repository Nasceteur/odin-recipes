Fugazzetta Recipe

Fugazzetta an Argentine classic

The Fugazzetta is an original pizza from Buenos Aires, Argentina noteworthy for the amount of cheese, onion and especially for being filled with cheese between two layers of dough

The Fugazzetta has its roots from the Italian community from Argentina being a combination of characteristics from the Neapolitan pizza and the Focaccia. It was made by the Argentine pizza maker Juan Banchero, son of genoves, in the neighborhood of La Boca between the 1893 and 1932 with the inauguration of the Banchero pizzeria

Ingredients

  • 500 g of wheat flour.
  • 10 g of salt.
  • 10 g of sugar.
  • 25 g of fresh yeast or 7 g of dry yeast.
  • 300 ml of warm water.
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • 500 g of grated mozzarella cheese.
  • 2 big onion, thinly sliced.
  • 2 tablespoons of oregano.
  • salt or pepper to taste.
  • Olive oil for sautéing the onions.

Steps

  1. In a big bowl, mix the flour with the salt and the sugar.
  2. Dissolve the fresh yeast in the warm water. (If you use dry yeast, mix it directly with the flour).
  3. Make a hole in the center of the flour mixture and add the water with the yeast, along with the two tablespoons fo olive oil.
  4. Mix well until you have a smooth, even dough
  5. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for 10 minutes, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  6. Put the dough in lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a wet cloth and let it rest for approximately 30 minutes, or until it doubles in size.
  7. Meanwhile, sauté the onions in a pan with a little bit of olive oil until they are golden brown. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
  9. Divide de dough in two parts. Stretch one of them and put it in a baking tray previously oiled.
  10. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the stretched dough.
  11. Cover it with the other part of dough and make pressure on the edges to seal it.
  12. Put the browned onions on the superior dough, covering the entire surface.
  13. Sprinkle over it the oregano.
  14. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 20 minutes or until the dough is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  15. Remove it from the oven and let it rest for some minutes and then cut it and serve it.
